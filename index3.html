Gameplay

  • The game starts with the turtle at the center of the screen.
  • The player enters a string length (between 1 and 10) and an angle (0, 90, 180, 270, or 360 degrees) using the input fields provided.
  • he game generates the Thue-Morse sequence of the given length and uses it to determine the turtle's moves
  • Each move corresponds to a segment of the turtle's path. The turtle moves forward by a fixed distance, and then turns by the given angle based on the current value of the Thue-Morse sequence
  • The game continues until the turtle has completed all of its moves.
